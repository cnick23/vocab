How to Play

01
Read the clue
Each word comes with up to 6 clues, revealed one at a time. Earlier clues are worth more points.
02
Guess the word
Type your answer and hit Guess. You have a limited pool of guesses per word — use them wisely.
03
Earn points & build streaks
Correct answers on early clues score big. Keep your streak alive to maximize your score.
04
Difficulty adapts
The game tracks your performance and adjusts automatically. Miss a word? It'll come back as a Grudge Match.
05
Guess pools by difficulty
